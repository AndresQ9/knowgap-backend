Privacy Policy Last updated: 7/15/25 KnowGap for Canvas (“the Extension”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension. 1. Information We Collect The Extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from users. The Extension interacts with Canvas and related educational platforms only to provide its intended functionality. No user data is sent to third-party servers except as required for the extension’s operation (e.g., communicating with your institution’s Canvas instance or the extension’s backend for video recommendations). 2. Use of Information Any information accessed by the Extension is used solely to provide features such as risk identification and video recommendations. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. 3. Data Security The Extension does not store any personal data outside of your local browser. Communications with external services (such as Canvas or the extension’s backend) are secured using HTTPS. 4. Third-Party Services The Extension may interact with third-party services (e.g., YouTube for video thumbnails). These services have their own privacy policies. 5. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
